 Kochi has a sparkling past when it was a nestling ground for Dutch, Portuguese and Chinese power and was the hub of spice trade in India. Sooner, the city got a lot of limelight and came to be known as one of the best tourist destinations in South India. From stunning coastline to vibrant culture, Kochi has a lot to offer to travelers. The hotels in Kochi are in no doubt absolutely gorgeous and offer amazing facilities with warm hospitality to guests. Some of them also offer amazing views from the terrace or balcony and also easy access to major attractions in Kochi. Let’s check out some of the best Kochi hotels that you can book for a great vacation in this port city.

Top 8 Hotels In Kochi For A Relaxing Retreat
There are so many good hotels in Kochi that not only offer some amazing facilities but also warm hospitality so that guests can feel like home away from home.

1. Grand Hyatt Kochi Bolgatty

One of the best Kochi hotels, Grand Hyatt Kochi Bolgatty is a five-star hotel that houses 264 elegantly designed rooms including 4 villas and 38 suites. Presidential suite, grand terrace suite, grand suite king, grand executive suite, two-bedroom and three-bedroom villa are some accommodation options to choose from. The hotel also takes care of your taste buds thus offer some amazing on-site options like Colony Clubhouse & Grill, Malabar Café, Thai Soul, and pool bar where you can relish snacks and drinks while taking a relaxing dip in the pool. Grand Hyatt Kochi is a waterfront hotel so guests can expect to enjoy beautiful scenery surrounding the hotel.

The hotel also takes care of the leisure and rejuvenation of the guests hence, one can enjoy spa, pool and fitness center within the premises of the hotel. There is also a spacious parking space that can accommodate up to 800 cars, Grand Club room and suite guests also gets free access to Grand Club Lounge.

2. Le Méridien Kochi

Le Méridien Kochi is one of the best five-star hotels in Kochi that offers a luxurious retreat to guests through its high-end facilities and exquisite interiors. The hotel is built in 18 acres of land and is surrounded by stunning backwaters of Kochi. It is also a great option for business travelers as the hotel features 60,000 square feet of event space that can accommodate around 5000 people. All the rooms and suites of the hotel are well-equipped with modern facilities like mini bar, coffee maker, a flat-screen TV, plush bedding and many other. The hotel is located at a prime located, close to some of the major attractions in Kochi like Kumarakom and Fort Kochi.

The hotel also features several multi-cuisine restaurants like Latest Recipe, Ember - The North Indian Restaurant, Marriott on Wheels by Le Meridien Kochi and Car-Go Bites. Guests can also indulge in leisure services offered like a revitalizing spa treatment, spacious fitness center with state-of-the-art equipment.

3. Radisson Blu Kochi

If you are looking for 5-star hotels in Cochin then Radisson Blu Kochi is a great option to book that features stylish interiors and offers warm hospitality to guests. The hotel is located in an ideal location close to amazing shopping places like LuLu Mall and MG Road and also just 20-minutes' drive from Willingdon Island which is one of the best islands in Kerala. What makes Radisson Blu one of the best Kochi hotels is its rooftop pool where you can take a dip, relax and enjoy enthralling views of the surrounding landscape.

Do not compromise on your fitness even if you are on a vacation at hotel’s fully-equipped spacious fitness center. The hotel also features four on-site restaurants serving local and international cuisines which are Keshia, United Nations of Asia, Ava- The Lounge Bar, and High Bar. It is also a great place for business travelers as meeting rooms, conference hall and boarding rooms are also available here.

4. Kochi Mariott Hotel

Kochi Marriot is one of the most elegant and modern hotels in Kochi, found in the metropolis area of the city. It defines comfort and luxury with its rooms, suites and upscale facilities along with 24-hour room service. Some of the facilities people can enjoy are the international cuisines served at the restaurant and lounges, pamper themselves at the spa, swim in the pool and work out in their fitness center. The hotel is complimented with its amazing backdrops ideal for meetings, social events and weddings along with the best catering services.

Bolgatty Palace to business and medical centers such Aster Medicity, Amrita Hospital, Cochin International Airport, the convention center and InfoPak are some of the main attractions and necessities around the hotel. The hotel also lies close to Cherai Beach, one of the best beaches in Kochi where some time in tranquility can be spent. The hotel ensures people have the most comfortable stay.

5. Forte Kochi

Bringing you close to the rich heritage of Kochi through its architecture, interiors and services, Forte Kochi is a 4-star boutique hotel that will take you through the rich past of Cochin and the times when Arab and Chinese traders used to come to this port city. The hotel is centrally located in the Princess Street and offers close access to some of the major attractions. One of the best things to do in Kochi is to go on a ferry ride and guests booking a stay in this hotel can easily enjoy it. The hotel features 27 traditionally designed rooms that are equipped with facilities like a mini bar, highspeed internet, hair dryer, free newspaper, coffee maker and several other facilities for a comfortable stay.

You can also enjoy the true local flavors of Kochi at the on-site restaurant located here serving breakfast, lunch and dinner. Poolside breakfast option is also available where you can relax in the cool waters of the pool while relishing scrumptious breakfast. If you are looking for cheap hotels in Kochi yet proper comfort and high-end facilities, then this should definitely be in your hitlist.
